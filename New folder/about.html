About Travelscape Maldives

Travelscape Maldives offers the highest standard and quality of personalized travel services and promises to make your holiday hassle-free. As a local island-based agency, we curate affordable, customizable, bespoke Maldivian experiences with world-class hospitality and no hidden charges.

Premium Excursions & Inbound Tours in the Maldives

Welcome to Travelscape Maldives, your premier local island-based gateway to bespoke Maldivian experiences. We emphasize inbound tours and undertake bookings from tour operators around the world at very competitive rates. We provide the best guest relations and guest transportation services with hospitality of excellence. We handle all hospitality-related business activities, including handling mega yachts and personal cruises, offering you an escape to a world of pristine beaches, azure waters, and luxurious indulgence.

Our vessels, including our modern Dhoni Speedboats, are skippered by experienced local captains and PADI certified instructors. We are committed to sustainable tourism, ensuring that our tours respect the marine environment and support local community partners. Start planning your dream getaway today and experience the Maldives like never before with our tailor-made excursions and itineraries.

Our Journey

Founded on the beautiful island of Maafushi, Travelscape Maldives began with a simple mission: to share the raw, untouched beauty of the Maldivian ocean with the world. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted provider of premium water sports, island hopping tours, and bespoke private charters. We pride ourselves on offering authentic, affordable, and customizable experiences that highlight the rich culture and vibrant marine life of the Kaafu Atoll, ensuring no hidden charges.

Every itinerary we design is crafted with care, emphasizing eco-friendly practices that protect our coral reefs. Our crew consists of lifelong islanders who know these waters better than anyone, ensuring that every dive, snorkel, and cruise is an unforgettable adventure into the deep blue.
